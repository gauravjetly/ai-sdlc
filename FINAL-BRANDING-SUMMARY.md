## 🔄 Important: Clear Your Browser Cache!

### Why?
Your browser may be caching the old colors and branding.

### How to Hard Refresh

**Chrome / Edge / Firefox:**
- Mac: `Cmd + Shift + R`
- Windows/Linux: `Ctrl + Shift + R`

**Safari:**
1. `Cmd + Option + E` (Empty cache)
2. `Cmd + R` (Reload)

**Or manually:**
1. Open DevTools (F12)
2. Right-click refresh button
3. Select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
