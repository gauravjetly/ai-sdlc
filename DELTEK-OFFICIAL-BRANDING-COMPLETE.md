## 🔄 Clear Browser Cache

**If you still see old colors:**

### Chrome/Edge
1. Press `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows)
2. Or: DevTools → Network → Disable cache

### Firefox
1. Press `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows)  
2. Or: Clear cache in Settings

### Safari
1. Press `Cmd+Option+E` to empty cache
2. Then `Cmd+R` to reload
